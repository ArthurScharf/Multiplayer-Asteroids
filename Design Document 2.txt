---- Game State ----

1. Waiting for player to connect
2. Timer starts upon first player connecting. Once timer elapses, game begins
3. Game Begins
4. Game loop
	- between 1-4 players are alive at any given time. 
	- Scoring & Timing is occurring
	- Game difficulty is increasing somehow
	- Respawn Timers are active. Players can respawn
5. All players are dead
6. Score is displayed and clients are disconnected
